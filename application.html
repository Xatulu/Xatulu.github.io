Who am I

I started at WACKER in 2010 as a student for mechanical engineering. During the 3 years with WACKER’s student program, I worked for both the Procurement and Logistics department as well as central engineering. After getting my Bachelor of Engineering, I started working in the Procurement’s eBusiness Team on different tasks - standard application support and project work as well as unconventional and upcoming topics like building and rolling out new tools. In 2016, I was taking on new challenges as Procurement Manager for IT Hardware and Infrastructure - up until July 2018. Since August, I have returned to my roots at Procurement’s Service division in our new “Procurement Systems Innovation” group. I am currently working on trending topics like the selection of the next generation of procurement tools, but also acting as a catalyst for new technologies - for example, one of my other projects is examining RPA’s usecases to further automate processes.
